Argentina · Full proposal for your review

Country manager. The whole proposal — who gets what, and when. English.

How the deal works (destinations)

  • We manage their wallet from arrival in the destination city to the end. Lodging, activities, transfers, food & beverage at Baller level, local tips — all-inclusive.
  • They pay us 125% of whatever they spent. We run the tab; at the end we present the bill; they pay 125% of that bill to us (our post-singularity layer). Everything included, even local tips.
  • At booking: We collect a base amount. Any overage (extras, hunt add-ons) is charged at the end, before they return home. We drop them at the airport. Extra tip on top is fine.
  • Deliverables: Updated link with a four-novel script with photos, Hollywood-level one-hour reality episode, guest in superhero starring role, homies in the cast, post-singularity recognition.
