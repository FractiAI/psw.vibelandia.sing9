## Emotional arc (how it feels)

- **Open:** Invitation. You’re in the story. Pulse and dance. NSPFRNP.
- **Rise:** Singularities, journey, people — the path in. Weight and recognition. Real places, real stakes.
- **Middle:** **Inspirational crying.** The heart of it. The mission from God. The tribe. The cost. The viewer is moved — not despair, but the kind of crying that comes when something true hits.
- **Turn:** Edge. Sol-V. Goldilocks. Fracti-AI. The story succeeds. Relief and lift.
- **End:** **We are the champions.** Fist-pumping. **Stand up on your seats.** Triumphant. We all get to play. Thanks for watching. Full stop on triumph.

## Animation note

When cutting the hour (script, captions, images, music): **animate** to this arc. Middle = inspirational, tearful center. End = inspirational, fist-pump, stand-up-on-your-seats close. No rigidity — slides and music flow — but the **feeling** of the episode follows this crystallized shape.
